ABOUT ME

NAVIGATING THE DIGITAL WORLD WITH THOUGHTFUL & INTELLIGENT DESIGN

My name is Corey and I am a full-stack, multidisciplinary designer with 10 years experience across all things digital. With a rich and diverse background in UX / UI, visual design, creative direction, brand development, front-end development and digital marketing, I have successfully delivered solutions to clients at companies such as Amazon, Microsoft, Accenture, F5, Zillow, Lyft, Trello and AutoDesk.

I am tool in a number of tools in technologies across the design and development space, combined to create the most meaningful and impactful experiences across web and product.

I am currently employed full-time as a Senior UX / UI Designer at Bodybuilding.com, focused on delivering polished, intuitive products and interfaces across mobile and web. As a recent transplant from Seattle, I enjoy avoiding traffic in my free-time, spending time with my wife, daughter and two cats, learning new technologies and soaking up as much sunshine as possible

Feel free to reach out if you're interested in kick-starting a project you've been holding off or just want to talk shop about design.
