ABOUT ME

NAVIGATING THE DIGITAL WORLD WITH THOUGHTFUL & INTELLIGENT DESIGN

My name is Corey and I am a full-stack, multidisciplinary designer with nearly 10 years experience across all things digital. With a background in visual design, UX / UI, creative direction, brand awareness, front-end development and digital marketing, I have successfully delivered solutions to clients at companies such as Amazon, Microsoft, Accenture, F5, Zillow, Lyft, Trello and AutoDesk as an extension of my freelance / agency experience or during my time owning my previous small-time agency, Five & Pine Co-Creative.

I am proficient in a number of tools in technologies across the design and development space, combined to create the most meaningful and impactful experiences for my customers across web and product.

I am currently employed full-time as a Product Manager and UX Designer at Ventive, focused on delivering polished, intuitive products and interfaces to clients. As a recent transplant from Seattle, I enjoy avoiding traffic in my free-time, spending time with my wife, daughter and two cats, learning new technologies and soaking up as much sunshine as possible

Feel free to reach out if you're interested in kick-starting a project you've been holding off or just want to talk shop about design. I'm currently accepting new clients as an extension of my new design and development agency, Tilt., where I am the owner and Creative Director / Principal Designer. Let's get something started.
