FITCLUB GOLF

← Previous Next →

Project:
FitClub Website

Year:
2016

Team:
Lightboard

BACKGROUND

Developing client relationships with improved User Experience. Creating meaningful experiences.

This was a landing built for a client at Lightboard. The company sold custom built golf clubs, facilitating customer orders with their online club-editor tool. They wanted something that conveyed their company brand with a vision moving forward. We were asked to maintain most functionality, while updating the visual look moving forward.

We put together this initial, fully flushed page following a quick check-in to ensure we were headed in the right direction. Once we gathered feedback, we built off that, eventually ironing out the full page, complete with golf club customization preview, featured items, blog posts, and testimonials. Our goal here was to provide something modern, consistent and easy to implement.
